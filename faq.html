Frequently Asked Questions

What is CS2Smokes?
CS2Smokes is a platform dedicated to providing smoke guides for Counter-Strike 2 players to improve their gameplay.
How do I use the smoke guides?
Navigate to the Maps section, select a map, press on the desired area to smoke and follow the video instructions.
Is CS2Smokes free to use?
Yes, CS2Smokes is completely free to use for all players.
